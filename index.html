How It Works

This demo implements an offchain privacy-preserving cookie banner. When you click "Accept", a cryptographic proof is generated that verifies your consent without revealing your identity or storing your preferences on the server.

Privacy Guarantees

No tracking: Your consent preferences are never stored on the server. Only cryptographic commitments are stored in a Merkle tree.

No identity leakage: Your 32-byte identity secret never leaves your browser. Only proofs and commitments are sent to the server.

Double-spend prevention: Nullifiers prevent the same proof from being used twice. The server tracks used nullifiers to prevent replay attacks.

Reset capability: For testing, you can reset both client and server state by running resetBanner() in the browser console.
